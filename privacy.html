Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Woolfin Studio ("Woolfin Studio," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Our app is a focus and productivity experience in which a cat chef bakes desserts as you complete focus sessions. We design the Services to collect only the information reasonably necessary to provide and improve that experience.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Policy applies to information collected through the Services, including our app, website, customer support channels, and other interactions you have with us. It does not apply to third-party websites, services, or applications that we do not own or control, even if they are linked from our Services.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect the following categories of information:

Contact information. If you contact us for support, business inquiries, or privacy-related requests, we may collect details such as your email address and any information you choose to include in your message.

Focus and app activity information. We may collect information related to your use of the app, such as focus session duration, session completion status, in-app progress, settings, reward or dessert unlocks, streaks, and other activity needed to provide app features.

Device and technical information. We may collect technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, language, approximate region, crash reports, diagnostic data, and similar device identifiers necessary to maintain performance, security, and compatibility.

Support and communications. If you contact us, we may keep records of your request, correspondence, and any attachments or screenshots you provide.

Payment information. If you purchase subscriptions or paid features through an app store, the relevant platform provider processes your payment. We generally do not receive your full payment card details, but we may receive limited transaction data such as purchase status, subscription tier, or renewal information.

Permissions-based information. If you choose to enable optional features that rely on device permissions, such as notifications, calendar-related reminders, or local media/profile assets, we may access the relevant data only to provide that feature and in accordance with your device settings and consent choices.

3. How We Use Information

We may use personal information for the following purposes:

Provide and operate the Services. This includes creating and managing focus sessions, saving progress, syncing settings where available, showing rewards, managing subscriptions, and delivering requested features.

Improve and develop the Services. We use information to understand feature usage, fix bugs, analyze performance, test updates, and improve the user experience.

Communicate with you. We may use your information to respond to questions, provide support, send service-related notices, and share important updates about the Services.

Protect security and integrity. We may use information to detect abuse, enforce our terms, investigate fraud or security incidents, and keep the Services safe.

Comply with law. We may process information to satisfy legal, regulatory, accounting, tax, or compliance obligations.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis for processing, we rely on one or more of the following: performance of a contract with you, our legitimate interests in operating and improving the Services, your consent, and compliance with legal obligations.

5. Cookies, Analytics, and Similar Technologies

Our website and app may use cookies, local storage, SDKs, analytics tools, or similar technologies to remember preferences, keep the Services working, understand usage patterns, and improve reliability. Where required by law, we will request consent before using non-essential technologies. You can also control certain technologies through your browser or device settings.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers. We may share information with vendors who help us host, store, analyze, secure, support, or operate the Services on our behalf, subject to contractual protections.

Platform providers. If you download the app through an app marketplace, relevant platform providers may process certain data, including subscription and transaction information, under their own privacy terms.

Legal and safety reasons. We may disclose information if required to do so by law or if we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, investigate fraud, respond to lawful requests, or prevent harm.

Business transfers. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, financing, or sale of assets, information may be disclosed as part of that transaction subject to applicable confidentiality and legal safeguards.

7. Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including providing the Services, maintaining records, resolving disputes, enforcing agreements, and complying with legal obligations. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the reason it was collected.

If you request deletion of personal information we hold about you, we will delete or de-identify relevant information within a reasonable period unless we are legally required or otherwise permitted to retain certain records, such as records related to completed transactions, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Depending on where you use the Services, your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps designed to provide an appropriate level of protection for cross-border transfers in accordance with applicable law.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent where processing is based on consent.

You may also control certain information directly through the app or device settings, such as notification permissions, optional device permissions, and cookie preferences where available.

To exercise applicable rights or make a privacy-related request, please contact us at woolfinstudio@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before completing certain requests.

The Services are not directed to children under the age at which parental consent is required under applicable law, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information in violation of applicable law, please contact us so that we can take appropriate steps.
