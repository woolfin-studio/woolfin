A tiny kitchen for better focus

Woolfin Studio creates a cozy focus app where a little cat chef bakes desserts while you stay with your task. Each session is designed to feel gentle, playful, and rewarding.

Instead of treating focus like pressure, we want it to feel like a warm routine. You bring your attention, and the cat chef turns that time into cakes, cookies, and small moments of progress worth celebrating.
